Scrambled Eggs

Eggs are one of my favorite food. Here is the recipe for deliciously rich scrambled eggs.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1tbs butter
  • 2tbs cream

Method

  1. Melt butter in a frying pan over a medium heat.
  2. Gently mix the egg and cream in a bowl.
  3. Once butter has melted, add cream and egg.
  4. Using spatula fold the egg from the edge of the pan to the center every 20 seconds(as if you are making an omlette).
  5. When the eggs are still moist remove from the heat(it will continue to cook on the plate until it is served).
